GeneralInformation

Set in London, St. Pancras Apartments by Citadel offers city views and free WiFi, 400 m from King's Cross Theatre and 1.4 km from British Museum.

The accommodation comes with a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath or shower. An oven, a microwave and toaster are also offered, as well as a kettle.
Dominion Theatre is 1.7 km from the apartment, while Camden Market is 1.9 km away. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 12 km from the property.
